HOW TO COLLECT (APPROACH & METHODOLOGY)

  • SOFT APPROACH

    Auditing, investigation, and counseling are done at this stage. We place phone calls and send demand letter to the debtor, in order to make debtor aware there is a collection problem and that third party is now

  • STRONG APPROACH

    We then place phone calls, send strong demand letters and physical field visit indicating serious action will be taken if the debt is not retired away. There is a sense of urgency in getting this account promptly paid.

  • ARRANGEMENT TO PAY

    We attempt to arrange, resolve any dispute and obtain clear commitment on debt resolution.

  • PERSISTENT AND INTOLERABLE

    After payment commitments being on place we work closely follow up and don’t give up until you get paid.

  • ATTORNEY & LITIGATION

    We involve internal solicitor to attempt to collect prior to litigation, if our efforts fails will consult you to see if it would be feasible to sue the debtor. If the sue is appropriate, we submit requirements for your approval. (We traditionally have less than 1% of all cases in legal process).
